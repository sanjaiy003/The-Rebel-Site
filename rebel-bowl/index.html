What is Rebel Bowl?

We will knock 39 RS off the price of your salad each time you use a Rebel Bowl instead of a disposable takeout container. Rebel Bowls are made of bamboo fibre. The bowl can be washed and used 100’s of times but can also be composted at the end of its useful life. Pretty cool!

Trying to keep
healthy?

Bring your Rebel Bowl with you to your favourite Green Rebel location and we’ll fill it with a fresh, delicious salad!
